Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site plan is to create a roadmap for the design of interactive trading website, which will operate as a virtual market place, where subscribers can display wares on their respective pages and woo potential clients or buyers to patronize them.

Audience

The target audience are young business oriented persons who cannot find convenience in the physical market places to carry out trading, business owners and service providers will also be accommodated in the website, there will also be social platform to create friendship and smooth communication among subscribers. the website is expected to be accessible from desktops, laptops, tablets and mobile phone with secure payment platforms.
